Features

What Spokn does.

The shortest path from speech to clipboard. That's the whole app.

Dual-engine transcription

Pick your mode in Settings. Auto uses OpenAI online, on-device offline. Online only always uses OpenAI. Local only keeps audio on device — nothing ever leaves your iPhone.

Your key, your bill

Bring your own OpenAI API key. Spokn stores it in the iOS Keychain and uses it to hit the transcription API directly. No markup, no reselling, no middleman.

Vocabulary hints

Save a list of names, jargon, or project terms in Settings. Spokn passes them to the transcription engine so "Kubernetes" stops becoming "coup or nets".

No accounts, no tracking

There's no sign-up, no server to talk to, no telemetry. Audio files are deleted the moment the transcript is ready. Uninstall removes everything.

Privacy

Three modes. Pick how much leaves your device.

You're in charge of the trade-off between accuracy and privacy — not us.

Auto

OpenAI when online, on-device when offline. The default. Best accuracy when the network's good, still works on a plane.

Online only

Always routes to OpenAI's /v1/audio/transcriptions. Highest accuracy. Requires your API key.

Local only

Uses WhisperKit on-device. Audio never leaves your iPhone. No key needed. Works fully offline.

Full details in the Privacy Policy.
