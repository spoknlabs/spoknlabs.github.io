Features

Built for the quickest path to text.

One trigger. One transcript. No extra ceremony.

Action Button on iPhone

Press once to start, press again to stop. The transcript lands on your clipboard, ready to paste anywhere.

Globe key on Mac

Hold Globe or Fn, speak, release. Spokn pastes at the cursor, or falls back to the clipboard when needed.

Your own OpenAI key

If you want online transcription, Spokn talks directly to OpenAI with your key. No proxy, no markup, no middleman.

On-device fallback

Spokn keeps a local fallback ready so dictation can stay on device when the online path is unavailable.
