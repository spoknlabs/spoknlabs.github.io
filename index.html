Values

What we care about.

Privacy by default

We don't run a backend, collect analytics, or build profiles. Your data lives on your device. When it has to leave, you pick the destination.

Fast, native

Native Swift. No Electron, no webview wrappers. Our apps boot instantly, sip battery, and feel like part of the OS because they are.

Quietly crafted

We sweat the small things: type, motion, shadow, the way a button feels at 3am. The result is software that gets out of your way.
